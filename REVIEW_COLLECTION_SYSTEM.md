# REVIEW COLLECTION SYSTEM

## Goal

Create a sustainable review generation process.

---

# Important Principles

Reviews should:
- feel authentic
- feel voluntary
- feel human

Avoid:
- aggressive automation
- fake reviews
- incentivized reviews

---

# Best Review Timing

Request reviews:
- after successful visa completion
- after positive support moments
- after strong communication experiences

---

# Review Request Channels

Potential:
- WhatsApp
- LINE
- email

---

# Ideal Review Style

Strong reviews often mention:
- responsiveness
- communication quality
- process clarity
- trust
- support quality

---

# Team Workflow

Process:
1. Successful support outcome
2. Follow-up message
3. Review request
4. Thank-you response

---

# Response Strategy

Respond to all reviews professionally.

Tone:
- appreciative
- human
- concise
- professional

---

# Long-Term Goal

Create:
- trust compounding
- local SEO growth
- review authority
- stronger conversion rates
