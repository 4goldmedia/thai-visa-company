# REVIEW RESPONSE GUIDELINES

## Tone

Responses should feel:
- appreciative
- professional
- human
- concise

Avoid:
- robotic wording
- corporate tone
- excessive marketing language

---

# Goals

Responses should:
- reinforce trust
- show responsiveness
- support reputation quality

---

# Good Response Structure

1. Thank reviewer
2. Reference experience
3. Reinforce support/appreciation

---

# Example Tone

"Thank you for your kind review. We're glad we could help make the visa process feel clearer and less stressful."

---

# Negative Reviews

Approach:
- remain calm
- acknowledge concerns
- offer offline resolution
- avoid defensiveness

Professionalism is critical.
