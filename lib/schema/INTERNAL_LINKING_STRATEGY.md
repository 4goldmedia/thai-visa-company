# INTERNAL LINKING STRATEGY

## Goal

Strengthen:
- topical authority
- SEO crawl depth
- AI-search understanding
- conversion paths

---

# Core Linking Structure

Homepage
→ Visa Pages
→ Resource Articles
→ Related Resources
→ Contact CTAs

---

# Visa Pages Should Link To

- relevant resource articles
- related visa pages
- FAQ content
- contact pages

---

# Articles Should Link To

- visa service pages
- related articles
- FAQ pages
- consultation/contact CTAs

---

# Related Resource Strategy

Every article should surface:
- 3–5 related articles
- related visa services

This strengthens:
- semantic topical clusters
- session depth
- crawl depth

---

# AI Search Optimization

Internal links should:
- use descriptive anchor text
- reinforce topic relationships
- clarify entity relevance

Avoid:
- generic "click here" links

---

# Conversion Path Strategy

Content should naturally guide users toward:
- LINE
- WhatsApp
- consultation requests

Without:
aggressive sales tactics.
