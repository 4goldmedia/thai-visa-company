# HOMEPAGE ARCHITECTURE

## Goal

The homepage should immediately communicate within 5 seconds:

- Thailand visa services
- simplicity
- trusted guidance
- fast communication
- modern professional experience
- 100% visa support

The homepage should feel:
- calm
- premium
- organized
- trustworthy
- easy to use

NOT:
- luxury agency
- complicated
- cluttered
- corporate bureaucracy

---

# Homepage Structure

## 1. Navbar

Purpose:
- simple navigation
- immediate trust
- quick communication access

Include:
- logo
- visa dropdown/services
- reviews
- blog/resources
- contact

CTA:
- LINE
- WhatsApp

Navbar should:
- remain minimal
- sticky on scroll
- transparent initially
- solid background on scroll

---

## 2. Hero Section

Purpose:
Immediate clarity.

User must understand:
- what the company does
- who it helps
- how to start

Hero should include:
- strong concise headline
- supporting copy
- trust indicators
- primary CTA
- secondary CTA
- review proof

Important:
Avoid oversized startup hero sections.

The hero should feel:
- efficient
- modern
- highly readable

Potential messaging:
"Thailand visa support without the confusion."

Trust elements:
- Google review rating
- response speed
- visa support expertise
- 100% assistance messaging

CTAs:
- Chat on LINE
- WhatsApp
- Free consultation

---

## 3. Visa Types Section

Purpose:
Quickly help users self-identify.

Show:
- Retirement Visa
- DTV Visa
- Elite Visa
- Business Visa
- Education Visa

Each card should include:
- short explanation
- key benefit
- simple CTA

This section should:
- reduce friction
- simplify discovery

---

## 4. Why Clients Choose Us

Purpose:
Trust and differentiation.

Focus on:
- fast communication
- simplified process
- trusted expertise
- support throughout process
- organized handling
- clear guidance

This should NOT feel like:
generic marketing bullet points.

---

## 5. Process Section

Purpose:
Reduce anxiety.

Simple 3-4 step process.

Examples:
1. Contact us
2. We review your situation
3. We prepare your visa process
4. Ongoing support

The process should feel:
- simple
- guided
- approachable

---

## 6. Reviews Section

Purpose:
Major trust builder.

Google reviews are extremely important.

This section should feel:
- authentic
- calm
- highly credible

Avoid:
- flashy testimonials
- fake startup-style quotes

Prioritize:
- screenshots
- review snippets
- review rating
- trust consistency

---

## 7. FAQ Preview

Purpose:
SEO + friction reduction.

Include practical questions:
- visa duration
- processing times
- required documents
- pricing expectations
- support questions

Answers should be:
- concise
- practical
- easy to scan

---

## 8. Blog / Resources Preview

Purpose:
SEO + expertise positioning.

Content should answer:
real user search intent.

Examples:
- Thailand Retirement Visa Requirements
- DTV Visa Thailand Explained
- Best Thai Visa Options
- Thai Visa Costs
- How Long Does a Thai Visa Take

Avoid generic SEO fluff.

---

## 9. Final CTA Section

Purpose:
High-conversion close.

Simple.
Minimal.
Clear.

Focus on:
- speaking to someone quickly
- low friction
- reassurance

Primary actions:
- LINE
- WhatsApp
- inquiry form

---

## 10. Footer

Purpose:
Trust + SEO structure.

Include:
- visa pages
- resources/blog
- company info
- contact details
- social links
- legal pages
- Google Business trust elements
